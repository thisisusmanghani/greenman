Product Definition

Multi-directional connectors are specialized components designed for
connecting strut channels in multiple directions, typically at 90

degrees and 180 degrees, to create strong and stable structural joints.
These brackets facilitate the assembly of custom frameworks by allowing
flexible alignment and angle combinations.
