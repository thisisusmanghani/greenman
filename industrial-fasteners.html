Anchoring Solutions

Greenman provides a comprehensive range of anchoring solutions designed for concrete & masonry connections, façade claddings, and industrial fixings. Our products are engineered to meet the rigorous demands of modern infrastructure projects.

Our Solutions Include:

  • Mechanical & Chemical Anchors: High-performance anchors for heavy-duty applications.
  • Façade-specific Brackets and Bolts: Specialized solutions for building envelopes.
  • High-tensile Fasteners and Nylon Plugs: Versatile fixing options for various substrates.

All our anchoring products are tested for tensile, shear, and seismic performance under ISO/ASTM guidelines, ensuring safety and reliability in critical applications.

Sealants & Sealing Solutions

Complementing our hardware, we offer advanced sealing solutions for HVAC ducts, façades, expansion joints, and fire-rated assemblies.

  • Silicone, PU, and duct sealants
  • Intumescent fire sealants (UL-certified)
  • Weatherproof and thermally stable foams

Our sealants meet SMACNA, UL94, and ASTM C920 standards.

Global Standards

We design all products in accordance with ISO, ASTM, DIN, SMACNA, FM and UL standards — ready for seismic, fire-safe, and energy-efficient infrastructure worldwide. Our solutions strike the balance between affordability and performance, ensuring compliance without sacrificing safety or strength.
