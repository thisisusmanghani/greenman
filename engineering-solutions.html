Engineering & Design Services

Greenman offers more than just products; we provide complete engineering and design services to support your project from concept to completion. Our team works closely with contractors to optimize designs for efficient assembly and structural integrity.

Our Services Include:

  • MEP Support Layouts: Optimized planning for mechanical, electrical, and plumbing supports.
  • Seismic Anchoring Design: Calculations and designs compliant with ETA TR029/TR045.
  • Solar System Design: Using advanced tools like CYPE & RAMA for precise structural analysis.
  • Off-site Modular Assemblies: Designing for rapid deployment and reduced on-site labor.

Digital Transformation & Smart Construction

Greenman is at the frontier of Industry 4.0. We leverage advanced technologies to enhance our engineering capabilities:

  • BIM-Ready Designs: We provide BIM-ready 3D models to support global construction workflows and clash detection.
  • Digital Twins: Simulating system behavior under stress to ensure performance before installation.
  • Online Configurators: Tools to help you select and design the right support system for your needs.

Solar Installation Systems

Our engineering expertise extends to renewable energy. We design weld-free, bolt-on modular frames for rooftop, ground-mount, and utility-scale solar projects. These structures are wind-load tested and designed for specific terrains and climates.
