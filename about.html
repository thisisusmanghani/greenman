About Us

Greenman is the flagship brand of Gladiator Building Products, a leader in engineered fastening and electromechanical support systems. With decades of industry expertise and state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities, we specialize in converting high-grade metals into precision-engineered components used across HVAC installations, plumbing infrastructure, industrial piping networks, mechanical installations, and comprehensive structural support systems. Our extensive product portfolio serves contractors, engineers, and construction professionals worldwide, providing them with reliable solutions for even the most demanding applications.

Every product we manufacture adheres to strict international compliance frameworks including ISO quality management standards, ASTM material specifications, DIN precision engineering standards, and EN European conformity requirements. This unwavering commitment ensures that each component delivers exceptional durability, unmatched reliability.

Mission

To empower infrastructure development across global markets by delivering compliant, precision-engineered fastening and support systems that offer exceptional strength, seamless scalability, and consistent quality. We are committed to supporting builders and contractors with innovative solutions that meet international standards while driving efficiency and reliability in every project we serve.

Vision

To become the world's most trusted and innovative provider of modular electromechanical systems, enabling faster, smarter, and greener construction practices across emerging and developed economies. We envision a future where our solutions set the global benchmark for quality, sustainability, and technological advancement in the building products industry.
